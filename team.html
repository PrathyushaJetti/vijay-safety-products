Mentor

Shri K.R. Sachdeva

Late Shri K.R. Sachdeva started his business of manufacturing rubber gloves & allied items in the year 1975. His technical expertise and his unique ability to apply his skills, has spearheaded the success behind launch of a wide range of highly electrical safety gloves in the Indian market.

His unwavering focus on product quality, leadership abilities, and deep knowledge of the subject have established Mr. Sachdeva as a prominent figure in this field.

Under Mr. Sachdeva’s leadership, Meera Rubber International has consistently prioritised quality and innovation. By investing in state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities and embracing latest technology, he ensured that every product meets the highest standards of performance and durability. This commitment to excellence has earned the company numerous accolades and a reputation for reliability.

Mr. Gautam Sachdeva

A second generation with over 30 years of experience in Safety and Personal Protective Equipment, Mr. Gautam has demonstrated visionary and purposeful leadership. He began his career by supplying rubber gloves primarily to industrial customers, gaining a deep understanding of their needs and maintaining a dedicated pursuit of excellence.

Collaborating with his father, Late Shri K.R. Sachdeva, Mr. Gautam transformed Meera Rubber International into a comprehensive source for rubber gloves and related products. His personal and marketing skills fostered trust and enduring relationships not only with customers across India but also with his team members.

In 2005, the company under his guidance diversified their portfolio by entering the broader field of Personal Protective Equipment under the name EMTEX SAFETY DEVICES. Since then, the company has become a leading name in PPE, offering a wide range of products including Hand Protection, Head Protection, Fall Protection, Ear Protection, Respiratory Protection, Eye Protection, Emergency Showers, and Road Safety Items.

Mr. Bhavik Sachdeva

Third generation leader Mr Bhavik Sachdeva an undergraduate student of IILM Lodhi Road join his family business, blending tradition with innovation. Passionate about sustaining the legacy while driving modern growth and to embrace new technologies, and maintaining strong family values. Bhavik's expertise in digital marketing and e-commerce brings a youthful viewpoint to the family business. His skills will be crucial as the company navigates the evolving business world. He contributes in reaching new customers and solidifying the company's online presence.
